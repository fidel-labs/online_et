About Us

Scalable Learning Solutions

Fidel LMS was built by Fidel Labs PLC.  Fidel Labs was founded in 2020 with a single mission: to build digital solutions that improve human lives. We approach each of our clients and projects with fresh eyes to develop customised, unique strategies.

We're a team of technology enthusiast returnees from the US, UK and Europe after long stints in helping build, manage and market technology for various Fortune 500 companies. Following our return to Home 1.0 (the Motherland aka Ethiopia) we embarked upon a mission to find a way of leveraging the knowledge and experiences we've gained to help solve challenges using technology as our enabler.

Our team is made up of specialists in Software Engineering, Programming, Digital Marketing, Big Data and Analytics.
